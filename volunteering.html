Community Engagement & Volunteer Work

I am passionate in serving my local community through spreading my love of Computer Science to those around me, as well as helping many beneficiaries with their basic needs and necessities. I have been involved in many community engagement projects and events, both in Computing events and non-Computing events.

Here is a list of community service and volunteer projects I have participated in.

Computer Science-related

  • BuildingBloCS 2019 8 Jun 2019
    An event aiming to spread the love of Computer Science to its participants through workshops and talks from industry partners. Participants were students from local secondary schools. I facilitated and led a group of 4 students to participate in the activities throughout the event.
  • SoC FLAG Beneficiary Engagement Day 8 & 25 Jul 2022
    An event where volunteers from NUS SoC engage beneficiaries from MINDS Fernvale Gardens School through teaching simple programming through Scratch (8 Jul) and a flower-making workshop (25 Jul). Part of SoC's FLAG initiative in 2022.

Non-Computer Science-related

  • Reach Out Apr 2022 – Present
    Reach Out is an organisation aiming to help the elderly and needy in rental flats all around Singapore by providing pre-loved goods, food and other necessities donated by others. Events are held on average twice a month on Sundays. I have been volunteering with this organisation nearly consistently and joined most events since Apr 2022. It also has programmes to help migrant worker communities in Singapore. Here is the link to Reach Out's official Facebook group page.
  • SoC FLAG Day 2022 1 Aug 2022
    FLAG is an initiative by NUS to help beneficiaries in partner organisations through engagement events and Flag Day. NUS School of Computing partnered with MINDS Fernvale Gardens School and this Flag Day aimed to raise funds for the school through public donations physically and online.
  • NUS CSC Grant-A-Wish XIX Wish Party 9 Jun 2022
    An event held by NUS Students' Community Service Club (NUS CSC). Prior to this event, the beneficiaries, who were primary school children, chose gifts to be granted to them by NUS students. During this event, the children played several game stations before being presented with their gifts. I was a volunteer for the first event on 9 Jun, engaging a group of 3 children there.
  • Keat Hong Edusave & CDC Awards Presentation Ceremony 4 Jan 2020
    Helped out with registration of students (awardees) and presentation of awards to them.
  • Keat Hong CC New Year's Day Celebrations 2020 31 Dec 2019 – 1 Jan 2020
    Helped with setting up and manning of game stations there.
  • AVIVA Superfundae 2019 15 Jun 2019
    Helped with setting up and manning of a game station there.
  • Project SPHERE with HDB May 2019
    An event, held in conjunction with Singapore's Housing and Development Board (HDB), where students and other volunteers help to do spring-cleaning for seniors living in rental flats. For my JC, we did the spring cleaning in the Teck Whye Lane estate. More info about Project SPHERE here.
